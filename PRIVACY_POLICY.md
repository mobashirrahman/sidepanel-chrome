# Privacy Policy for AI Sidekick

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview
AI Sidekick is a Google Chrome Extension designed to enhance your productivity by providing an all-in-one multitasking sidebar. We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to keeping your data secure and entirely local.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
AI Sidekick **does not** collect, transmit, store externally, or share any personal user data. We do not have any external servers that receive data from your use of the extension.

## 2. Local Storage
All data generated by your use of AI Sidekick is stored locally on your device using the `chrome.storage.local` API. This includes:
- Your pinned websites and their custom URLs.
- Your preferences (such as your chosen default AI provider, search engine, and app visibility settings).
- Any notes, text, or files you save using the built-in "Drop" feature.

If you uninstall the extension, this locally stored data is automatically deleted by your browser.

## 3. Required Permissions and Justification
AI Sidekick requires certain permissions to function correctly:

- **`storage`**: Used exclusively to save your local settings, pinned sites, and Drop notes on your computer.
- **`sidePanel`**: Required to display the extension interface in the Chrome side panel.
- **`tabs`**: Used to identify the URL of your active tab when you click "Pin Current Active Tab". We do not track your browsing history.
- **`<all_urls>`, `declarativeNetRequest`, and `declarativeNetRequestWithHostAccess`**: AI Sidekick allows you to pin and view *any* website of your choosing within the sidebar iframe. Many websites employ security headers (`X-Frame-Options` and `Content-Security-Policy`) that block them from being loaded inside an iframe. These permissions are strictly used to programmatically remove those framing restrictions for the specific sites you pin, enabling them to render correctly in the side panel.

## 4. Third-Party Links and Services
The extension provides shortcuts to third-party services (such as ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, Bing, etc.). These are essentially bookmarks that load the official websites within the side panel. 
Your interactions with these third-party AI providers or search engines are governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service. AI Sidekick does not intercept, read, or log any queries or responses you exchange with these services.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the support link on the Chrome Web Store listing.
